PIBIT.AI

The Decision Layer Prototype: Turning DocumentCURE extractions into automated underwriting risk intelligence.

THE THESIS

Pibit.ai's DocumentCURE is highly capable of parsing unstructured ACORD forms into JSON. But extraction is quickly becoming a commodity war among AI startups.

The true defensible moat is the Decision Layer (RiskCURE). Having managed 63L+ policies at ICICI Prudential, I know underwriters don't just want clean data—they want to know if they should write the risk.

I built a Python prototype that simulates this exact decision layer. It ingests the JSON, cross-references 5 critical property underwriting vectors, and automatically flags compound risks.
